The perfect basecamp for adventure.

Camp Alder is our passion project and has been years in the making. We hope you all love it and treat it like home just as we do. This quiet getaway features luxury furnishing and a cozy wood stove. The cabin is nestled among soaring Douglas firs on a large, private lot. It's just 5 miles away from Mt. Rainier Nisqually entrance. It's close enough to beat the crowds early in the morning but not close enough to be blocked by the queue into the park on busier days. Camp Alder is an ideal location to get the most out of your day in the park.
